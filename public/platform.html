SenseLive Industrial IoT & AI Platform

A full stack hardware-software IoT and AI platform to connect any industrial product or machinery and get visibility on the entire factory in a unified way.

What makes SenseLive unique

We connect any industrial machinery in less than 3 hours, with maximum security

Plug-and-play Industrial IoT platform with preconfigured Apps, microcontroller-based technology and integrated Secure Element.

How does it work

  1. Connect all your machinery using Edge Devices or through Agent. If needed add sensors to the machines to collect all data you need.
  2. Collect and elaborate data on the edge, send cleaned data to the Cloud.
  3. Organize data and elaborate them with AI algorithms to get KPIs..
  4. Integrate the Zerynth Platform and the data with the other IT tools you have in the company.
  5. Visualize KPIs on your Apps, get alerts and make data-driven decisions.
