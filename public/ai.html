How does it work?

The Senselive Industrial IoT Platform can connect with any asset in a simple and non-invasive way thanks to the Edge AI Layer. This includes both legacy and modern machines, which can be connected through SenseLive Edge Devices or Agents installed on the machine or on the corporate IT/OT infrastructure.
